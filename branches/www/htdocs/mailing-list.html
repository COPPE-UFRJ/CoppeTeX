Mailing List

We have a mailing list where you can ask the CoppeTeX team and users about any problem you are experiencing. It is also used to post administrators' announcements to the community. To know how to send messages and how to subscribe or unsubscribe the list, read the sections below.

Subscribe/Unsubscribe

You can subscribe the CoppeTeX-users mailing list following the instructions given at the page:

https://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/coppetex-users

If you are fed up of receiving our messages, you have also to point the browser to the link above. There, it is still possible to get a password reminder or change your subscription options.

Sending a message

To post a message to all list members, send an email to:

coppetex-users at lists.sourceforge.net.

But before doing this, you should check the list archives located at CoppeTeX-users Archives.
